// about

I've spent 12+ years building iOS applications that people actually depend on — from enterprise mobility at Deloitte to cross-platform fintech at hedgehog lab to, now, Bluetooth SDKs that connect hearing aids to the world.

My work lives at the low level — CoreBluetooth state machines, GATT profiles, reverse-engineering proprietary device protocols, and squeezing reliability out of radios that don't want to cooperate. Every line of code I write helps someone hear better.

Off the clock I'm building OmWhisper — a macOS voice-transcription app powered by fully on-device AI (whisper.cpp), built from scratch in Rust + Tauri. No cloud. No telemetry. I don't just explore new tech — I ship it.
